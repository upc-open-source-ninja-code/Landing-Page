About us

NinjaCode is a startup made up of students from the Faculty of Engineering and Sciences of the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences (UPC), dedicated to optimizing medical follow-ups. Our vision is to address the existing challenges in the Peruvian healthcare system, especially in the monitoring of hormonal diseases.
